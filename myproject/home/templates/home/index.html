Welcome to my website, built with the help of Django, jQuery, Backbone.js and HTMl5 Boilerplate.

I built this website with the hope of collating some of the projects I've worked on, as well as to learn about Web dev.

You can find the various projects in the nav bar on the left.


Below is an example of one, a physics engine I've built in Javascript. At the moment, there are particles that rebound according to the laws of momentum and springs that work according to Hooke's Law. The simulation below is just an example, with a Newton cradle (including energy loss), a damped spring and some perfectly elastic particles at the bottom.


It's also interactive.

Click a particle to "kick" it or click and drag to pull it somewhere.

The damped spring only takes so much tension: if you pull it too hard it'll break. The cradle springs however, will hardly extend at all and won't break.
